What is CulturaX?

CulturaX is a digital bridge to India’s heritage.

The platform captures monuments as detailed 3D and AR experiences, wrapping them in guided narratives and quizzes so that students, tourists, and lifelong learners can explore history in an engaging, self-paced way.

The problem CulturaX addresses

Many heritage sites face erosion, overcrowding, and uneven access. Classrooms often rely on static images or text, and travel is not always possible for remote communities.

  • Physical monuments are vulnerable to time, weather, and mass tourism.
  • Students outside major cities rarely get meaningful site visits.
  • Conventional teaching materials struggle to spark curiosity.
  • There is limited infrastructure for digital preservation at scale.

Impact & Benefits

How CulturaX creates lasting value.

Cultural Impact

  • Digitally preserves Indian monuments in high fidelity for future generations.
  • Reduces the risk of heritage loss from erosion, damage, climate and age.
  • Extends cultural awareness and curiosity across younger audiences.

Educational Impact

  • Delivers interactive, AR-based learning experiences for schools and colleges.
  • Makes history memorable through 3D visuals, narratives and interactive quizzes.
  • Provides rich learning content to remote communities without travel barriers.

Technological Impact

  • Introduces learners to STEM concepts through AR, 3D modelling and real-time graphics.
  • Opens up new career paths in digital heritage, scanning, AR and content design.
  • Encourages the adoption of immersive technologies in mainstream education.

Tourism Impact

  • Enhances on-site visits with AR overlays and contextual storytelling.
  • Acts as a virtual tourism gateway for audiences who cannot travel in person.
  • Strengthens cultural tourism through high-quality digital outreach.

Social Impact

  • Makes India’s cultural heritage accessible to anyone, anywhere in the world.
  • Builds a sense of pride and connection with historical identity.
  • Promotes digital inclusion by reaching learners across diverse regions.
